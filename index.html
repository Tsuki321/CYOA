The Mages Association Bureau

A Magical Awakening Awaits

In the shadows of governmental secrecy, humanity discovered the impossible. What began as desperation became revolution. What was meant to save a nation became the gateway to a new reality.

Twelve years ago, the United States initiated the Star Light Project under Executive Directive SL-001. China's rapid technological and economic ascension had created a strategic imbalance that conventional military and economic countermeasures could not address. The directive allocated $47 billion in black budget funding toward experimental research into asymmetric technological advantages. DARPA assumed operational control, establishing Site-7 beneath the Nevada Test Range and recruiting from the upper echelons of theoretical physics, quantum mechanics, and dimensional mathematics.

The research team—designated Helix Group—operated under compartmentalized clearance protocols that isolated them from conventional oversight. For three years, they pursued theoretical frameworks in higher-dimensional physics, generating over two thousand pages of mathematical models while burning through 68% of allocated funding with no tangible results. DARPA oversight considered program termination twice. Then, on October 31st, 2015, Dr. Sarah Chen's dimensional resonance scanner detected an anomaly during a routine calibration test.

The sensors registered energy signatures emanating from dimensions beyond conventional spacetime. This Higher Dimensional Energy permeated all of reality, existing in spaces that conventional matter could not reach yet demonstrating measurable interaction with standard-model particles under specific conditions. Within six months, DARPA had verified the discovery across three independent facilities. By mid-2016, as the implications became clear, operational authority transferred to a newly established oversight body—the High Thaumaturgic Council—an organization of the world's most powerful mages designed to manage what had ceased to be a purely military research program and had become something that would reshape civilization.

Helix Group developed extraction and containment protocols that allowed controlled study of the energy, which they designated "Mana" after extensive debate over nomenclature. The term carried mythological baggage, but accurately conveyed the energy's responsive properties—it demonstrated measurable reaction to human consciousness and intent under laboratory conditions. When volunteer subjects were exposed to controlled concentrations of extracted Mana in sealed isolation chambers, the energy induced a physiological transformation that existing medical science could not fully explain.

The human body possessed a dormant organ that awakened upon Mana exposure. Located in the same spatial coordinates as the heart but existing partially in higher dimensions beyond standard spacetime, this organ—designated the Mana Reactor—became metabolically active when saturated with sufficient concentrations of Mana. The reactor existed in a superposition state, simultaneously present within the physical body and extending into dimensions that normal matter could not reach, serving as a dimensional bridge that allowed higher-dimensional energy to interact with baseline reality through a biological substrate.

Further research revealed that every human possessed this dimensional organ in a dormant state from birth, but potential capacity varied by nearly two orders of magnitude between individuals. Helix Group developed a classification system based on the Kabbalistic Tree of Life, ranging from Malkuth (baseline) to Keter (maximum observed capacity). This ranking determined mana storage capacity, ambient absorption rate, and conversion efficiency for dietary mana intake. Genetic screening and dimensional resonance imaging could identify an individual's rank with 94% accuracy, but no method existed to artificially enhance it.

Reactor rank was immutable, determined by developmental factors during gestation that current science could not replicate or modify. Training optimized reactor control and discharge efficiency, but fundamental capacity remained fixed. Keter-rank individuals demonstrated reactor capacities roughly 70 times baseline Malkuth capacity, with proportionally enhanced absorption and conversion rates. This created a strategic problem—the distribution of reactor ranks across the population was random, uncontrollable, and created a new axis of human capability that no existing social or military structure was designed to accommodate.

As research progressed, Helix Group encountered a critical limitation. While the Mana Reactor could store and generate dimensional energy, the human brain lacked the processing architecture to manipulate that energy into targeted effects in normal space. The calculations required to shape raw Mana into functional phenomena exceeded human cognitive capacity by three to four orders of magnitude. Early volunteers who attempted manual mana manipulation suffered severe neural degradation—seizures, hemorrhaging, and permanent cognitive damage from computational strain that biological neurons could not sustain.

The solution emerged from collaboration between Helix Group and DARPA's artificial intelligence division. They developed the Intelligent Device: a palm-sized magitech construct serving as an external processing unit for mana operations. Each device contained a specialized Artificial General Intelligence designed to handle the dimensional mathematics required for mana manipulation. A bonding protocol integrated the device with the user's genetic signature and their reactor's unique energy pattern, creating a neural interface that responded to conscious intent with sub-100-millisecond latency.

Without an Intelligent Device, awakened individuals were functionally powerless, capable of sensing mana but unable to shape it into measurable effects. The device translated user intent into Spell Routines—complex mathematical programs that manipulated mana to produce specific phenomena in baseline reality. These routines were classified using a ten-tier system ranking them by effective range, energy throughput, and computational complexity (referred to as "weave"). Tier 1 spells operated at room scale with minimal energy expenditure—lighting fires, generating illumination, basic kinetic manipulation. Tier 10 spells represented strategic weapons with city-scale ranges, megajoule energy outputs, and computational requirements that stressed even fourth-generation device architectures.

Intelligent Device technology has progressed through five distinct generations. First Generation devices, classified as Civilian-grade, provided basic spell routine execution suitable for everyday applications but lacked the processing power or material science for combat-grade operations. These devices formed the backbone of civilian magical infrastructure—64% of awakened individuals in the United States operate First Generation devices as of 2024. Second and Third Generation devices marked the transition to professional-grade equipment, incorporating hardened materials, enhanced processing architectures, and more sophisticated AGI systems capable of limited autonomous operation.

Fourth Generation devices represented the current cutting edge of mass-produced magical technology. They featured quantum-enhanced processing cores, direct neural interface capabilities, and AGI systems sophisticated enough for complex tactical planning and semi-autonomous operation in contested environments. Production costs limited Fourth Generation deployment primarily to elite military units and high-value corporate security applications. Fifth Generation devices remained experimental—fewer than thirty units existed worldwide, incorporating self-evolving AGI architectures, programmable metamaterials, and processing capabilities that approached theoretical limits imposed by dimensional physics. Access to Fifth Generation technology required Council authorization and served as a strategic asset in its own right.

When DARPA transferred operational authority over magical research in 2016, they did not hand control to bureaucrats or politicians. The nascent magical community had already produced individuals of extraordinary power—mages whose reactor capacities and combat capabilities exceeded anything conventional military force could contain. The solution was unprecedented: operational authority would rest with those who possessed the power to enforce their decisions. Thus formed the High Thaumaturgic Council, composed of ten seats, each held by one of the world's most powerful mages.

The Council's legal mandate placed them answerable only to the President of the United States, with authority to oversee all magical research, policy formulation, and strategic deployment of magical assets. However, the founding charter included an unprecedented provision: the Council retained constitutional authority to intervene should the President abuse executive power in magical matters, effectively making them a fourth branch of government for all matters concerning dimensional energy. This arrangement reflected a pragmatic reality—no conventional authority possessed the capability to compel obedience from a Keter-rank mage operating Fifth Generation equipment.

Council seats were not appointed through political process but earned through demonstrated capability, magical power, and contribution to dimensional research. The First Seat held final authority on all Council decisions, requiring a unanimous vote of the remaining nine seats to override. Each Council member maintained personal research programs, commanded substantial resources, and represented different specializations within magical theory and practice. Their decisions shaped everything from civilian awakening protocols to military deployment doctrines to the fundamental direction of magical research itself.

Council Composition

First Seat: Archmage Seraphine Winslow

Rank: Keter (70x baseline capacity, representing the practical ceiling of human magical potential)

Role: Council Chair, final authority on all magical policy and research direction

Specialization: Abstract/conceptual magic, higher-dimensional theory, reality manipulation

Device: Fifth Generation grimoire-type (designation: "Akashic Index")

Former lead theoretical physicist for Helix Group, Winslow became the first human to achieve Keter rank through a controlled rank-up event—a phenomenon where extreme magical stress during reactor development pushes capacity beyond genetic baseline. Her research into conceptual manipulation and dimensional mathematics remains classified at the highest levels. Winslow's combat capabilities remain largely theoretical; no peer-level opponent has survived engagement long enough to provide actionable intelligence. She approaches magical research with the precision of a physicist and the vision of a revolutionary, viewing current magical theory as merely the foundation for capabilities humanity has yet to imagine.

Second Seat: Master Chen Zhao

Rank: Chokhmah (90 PP)

Role: Director of Combat Applications and Military Integration

Specialization: Elemental magic, large-scale battlefield control, combined arms doctrine

Device: Fifth Generation staff-type (designation: "Heaven's Mandate")

Defector from the Chinese magical warfare program, Chen brought with him complete documentation of their research into mass combat applications of dimensional energy. His tactical acumen transformed magical warfare from individual dueling to coordinated combined-arms operations. Chen's elemental manipulation operates at strategic scale—he can generate weather systems, redirect rivers, or create firestorms that consume square kilometers.

Third Seat: Dr. Yuki Takamura

Rank: Binah (80 PP)

Role: Chief Medical Researcher, Biological Integration Specialist

Specialization: Healing magic, biological manipulation, reactor physiology

Device: Fifth Generation hybrid grimoire-scepter (designation: "Caduceus Protocol")

Former trauma surgeon who pioneered magical healing techniques that operate at the cellular level. Takamura's research into reactor biology enabled the controlled awakening protocols used worldwide. Her healing capabilities extend beyond mere tissue repair to genetic-level correction, disease eradication, and even limited age regression, though the ethical implications of the latter remain fiercely debated.

Fourth Seat: Commander Victor Reyes

Rank: Chesed (70 PP)

Role: Director of Magical Law Enforcement, MAB Oversight

Specialization: Anti-mage combat, suppression fields, investigative magic

Device: Fourth Generation armament-type (designation: "Justicar's Edge")

Former Navy SEAL who awakened during a classified operation, Reyes built the MAB from the ground up. His specialization in counter-magic techniques makes him uniquely dangerous to rogue mages despite his comparatively lower reactor rank. Reyes maintains that law applies equally to all awakened individuals regardless of power, a philosophy that has put him at odds with more radical Council members but earned grudging respect from the magical community.

Fifth Seat: Professor Amara Okonkwo

Rank: Geburah (60 PP)

Role: Director of Dimensional Research, Theoretical Applications Lead

Specialization: Spatial manipulation, teleportation, dimensional mathematics

Device: Fifth Generation calculation-type (designation: "Tesseract Engine")

Nigerian-born mathematician whose doctoral thesis on higher-dimensional topology became foundational to modern magical theory. Okonkwo's spatial manipulation capabilities enable instantaneous transportation across continental distances and creation of pocket dimensions for storage or containment. Her research into dimensional barriers led to breakthrough advancements in defensive magic that protect civilian population centers.

Sixth Seat: Magister Klaus Reinhardt

Rank: Chesed (70 PP)

Role: Director of International Relations, European Liaison

Specialization: Barrier construction, defensive arrays, strategic wards

Device: Fourth Generation armament-type (designation: "Aegis Eternal")

Former German GSG-9 operator who pioneered layered defensive ward systems that protect critical infrastructure. Reinhardt coordinates with European magical authorities to maintain the fragile international cooperation necessary for global magical stability. His defensive constructs are considered theoretically impenetrable below Tier 9 assault capabilities, making him invaluable for protecting high-value targets and secure facilities.

Seventh Seat: Archon Dmitri Volkov

Rank: Binah (80 PP)

Role: Director of Strategic Assets, Weapons Development Oversight

Specialization: Energy projection, strategic bombardment, weapons integration

Device: Fifth Generation artillery-type (designation: "Tsar Protocol")

Russian former nuclear physicist who transitioned to magical weapons development. Volkov's research into mana-based strategic weapons created alternatives to nuclear arsenals that offer equivalent destructive capability with far greater precision and control. His bombardment capabilities approach tactical nuclear yields but can be deployed with surgical precision, making him both essential to strategic deterrence and deeply feared by conventional military planners.

Eighth Seat: Sage Priya Sharma

Rank: Chokhmah (90 PP)

Role: Director of Civilian Integration, Public Awakening Programs

Specialization: Enhancement magic, mass ritual coordination, public education

Device: Fourth Generation support-type (designation: "Lotus Mandala")

Indian social scientist who revolutionized civilian magical education and safe awakening protocols. Sharma's research into group magical dynamics enabled the mass awakening programs that brought magic to hundreds of thousands safely. Her enhancement capabilities can temporarily elevate the performance of entire teams simultaneously, making her essential to large-scale magical operations that require coordination across dozens of participants.

Ninth Seat: Artificer Kenji Matsumoto

Rank: Geburah (60 PP)

Role: Director of Device Engineering, Manufacturing Oversight

Specialization: Magitech engineering, device optimization, artificial intelligence integration

Device: Fifth Generation custom crafting-type (designation: "Forge of Hephaestus")

Japanese robotics engineer who led the development of Third through Fifth Generation Intelligent Device architectures. Matsumoto's understanding of the intersection between magic and technology is unparalleled; he can analyze any device's construction and capabilities through direct examination and has personally crafted all experimental Sixth Generation prototypes. His work ensures that every awakened mage can access equipment appropriate to their capabilities.

Tenth Seat: Oracle Fatima Al-Rashid

Rank: Chesed (70 PP)

Role: Director of Intelligence Operations, Threat Assessment

Specialization: Divination, information warfare, predictive analysis

Device: Fourth Generation sensory-type (designation: "All-Seeing Eye")

Saudi intelligence analyst whose awakening manifested unprecedented divination capabilities. Al-Rashid's precognitive abilities operate through probability analysis and temporal resonance rather than deterministic future-sight, allowing her to identify likely threat vectors before they fully materialize. Her intelligence network spans the globe, combining magical surveillance with conventional tradecraft to maintain awareness of threats to magical stability. Every major Council decision passes through her threat assessment before implementation.

The High Thaumaturgic Council represents humanity's attempt to govern the ungovernable—to impose order on power that transcends conventional authority structures. Their decisions shape the fate of millions, their research pushes the boundaries of reality itself, and their very existence serves as both protection and warning. They are humanity's greatest defense against magical threats and, should they choose, its most terrifying danger. Every awakened mage exists within the framework they have created, subject to laws they enforce, using devices they approve, following protocols they establish. The Council is not simply an organization—it is the foundation upon which the entire magical world rests.

The revolution that changed everything came with the discovery that Intelligent Devices were not constrained to a single form. Through advanced shape-shifting protocols, these devices could reconfigure their physical structure into virtually any form the mage required, limited only by mass-energy conservation principles and the device's generation level. This transformative capability gave rise to specialized fighting styles and, eventually, to the formal classification of mages into three primary Classes based on their combat methodology and device configuration preferences.

The Knight class emerged as specialists in body reinforcement and melee combat, transforming their Intelligent Devices into bladed weapons that channeled and focused their magical power. Knights developed fighting techniques that combined traditional martial disciplines with supernatural enhancement, using Mana to strengthen their bodies to superhuman levels while wielding magically-enhanced weapons capable of cutting through reinforced steel like paper. A skilled Knight could move faster than the human eye could track, strike with enough force to demolish buildings, and endure attacks that would instantly kill unenhanced humans.

Their combat style emphasized explosive close-quarters engagement, overwhelming opponents through superior speed and devastating physical power before enemies could leverage the range advantage typical of magical combat. Knights excelled at assassination, rapid strike operations, and scenarios where collateral damage needed to be minimized through precise, surgical violence. The most accomplished Knights could face entire squads of conventional soldiers single-handedly, their enhanced reflexes and durability making them virtually unstoppable in close combat.

In stark contrast, Bombardment mages specialized in long-range magical warfare, transforming their Intelligent Devices into staffs, cannons, or other focusing implements for devastating ranged attacks. These mages represented the artillery of the magical world, capable of engaging targets from kilometers away with precision that made conventional weapons obsolete. Bombardment mages developed spell routines optimized for maximum destructive output, trading the Knight's mobility and resilience for overwhelming offensive power that could devastate entire battlefields.

A skilled Bombardment mage operating from a secure position could rain down destruction that rivaled conventional artillery bombardments, but with far greater accuracy and the ability to adjust their attacks in real-time. They excelled at siege warfare, area denial, and eliminating high-value targets from beyond retaliation range. The most accomplished Bombardment mages could level city blocks with single spell routines, create sustained barrages that overwhelmed defensive barriers through sheer volume, or execute precision strikes that eliminated specific targets without collateral damage.

The third class, Thaumaturges, specialized in support roles that proved absolutely essential to sustained magical operations. While Knights and Bombardment mages focused on direct combat, Thaumaturges excelled at force multiplication, healing, enhancement, and disruption of enemy systems. Their Intelligent Devices typically maintained the basic cube configuration or transformed into grimoires and books that served as repositories for vast spell routine libraries. Thaumaturges were force multipliers par excellence, capable of transforming mediocre squads into elite units through strategic enhancement and support.

A skilled Thaumaturge could heal injuries that would be fatal to conventional medicine, enhance allies' physical and magical capabilities beyond their normal limits, restore depleted Mana Reactors to full capacity, and even hack into enemy Intelligent Devices or technological systems through invasive spell routines. In military operations, Thaumaturges often proved more valuable than entire platoons of conventional soldiers, their ability to sustain and enhance allied forces while disrupting enemy capabilities making them primary targets for elimination.

Perhaps the most visible innovation in magical technology came with the development of Barrier Jackets—autonomous armor systems that provided both protection and personal expression. A Barrier Jacket activated when a mage circulated their Mana through their Intelligent Device while engaging a pre-programmed defensive spell routine. Unlike traditional armor, Barrier Jackets existed partially in higher dimensions, providing comprehensive protection against both physical and magical attacks while weighing virtually nothing and restricting movement not at all.

The appearance of a Barrier Jacket could be extensively customized, limited only by the mage's imagination and their Intelligent Device's processing capabilities. Some mages favored elegant ballroom gowns or Victorian formal wear that combined beauty with protection. Others preferred military uniforms that projected authority and discipline, or sleek tactical suits that prioritized functionality over aesthetics. The customization options were nearly limitless, allowing each mage to create a unique visual identity that reflected their personality, affiliations, and combat role.

Beyond mere aesthetics, Barrier Jackets provided substantial defensive capabilities and additional Mana storage. The magical barriers layered within the jacket's structure could absorb tremendous damage before failing, automatically repairing themselves as long as the mage maintained the spell routine. More advanced Barrier Jackets incorporated additional utility functions: environmental protection for operations in hazardous conditions, integrated communication systems, enhanced sensory arrays, or even limited flight capabilities through magical thrust generation.

The proliferation of magical capability among the general population created unprecedented challenges for law enforcement and national security. Within five years of the Star Light Project's success, over fifty thousand Americans had undergone Mana Reactor activation, with that number growing exponentially as activation protocols became standardized and widely available. The potential for catastrophic abuse of magical power by criminals, terrorists, or simply reckless individuals became a primary concern for government officials who understood the destructive capability even a single rogue mage could unleash.

In response, Congress authorized the creation of the Mages Association Bureau (MAB)—a federal agency operating with extraordinary powers and resources to regulate, monitor, and police the magical population. The MAB received federal jurisdiction that superseded state and local authority in magical matters, operating under classified directives that granted them latitude that would be unconstitutional for conventional law enforcement. Their primary mission was twofold: prevent magical knowledge from reaching the general public, and ensure that those who possessed magical capabilities could be monitored and controlled.

The MAB maintained an extensive network of agents drawn from both military special forces and the civilian magical population itself. Every individual who underwent Mana Reactor activation was required by federal law to register with the Bureau, providing detailed information about their rank, capabilities, and intended uses of magic. Failure to register or attempts to conceal magical capability were felonies carrying severe penalties including permanent imprisonment in specialized containment facilities designed to suppress magical abilities.

In addition to law enforcement, the MAB controlled all legal access to Intelligent Devices. Every device manufactured in the United States contained tracking systems and remote shutdown capabilities that the Bureau could activate if the registered user engaged in illegal magical activities. The Bureau maintained extensive databases tracking every registered mage, every Intelligent Device, and every legal spell routine, creating a comprehensive surveillance network that monitored the magical population with unprecedented thoroughness.

Despite its authoritarian aspects, the MAB also provided essential services to the magical community. They funded magical research, maintained educational programs for newly awakened mages, offered employment opportunities for those seeking to use their abilities in public service, and investigated magical crimes with expertise that conventional law enforcement could never match. The Bureau walked a fine line between protector and oppressor, maintaining order through a combination of overwhelming force, comprehensive surveillance, and strategic provision of resources that made cooperation more attractive than resistance.

All Intelligent Devices, regardless of generation, were produced under MAB oversight with features tailored to the mage's rank, role, and the resources available to them. Civilian mages typically received First or Second Generation devices suitable for everyday magical tasks. Professional mages in law enforcement, private security, or corporate research roles accessed Third Generation devices. Military personnel and MAB agents operated Fourth Generation equipment. Fifth Generation devices remained exclusively under Bureau control, issued only to their most elite operatives or experimental research programs.
